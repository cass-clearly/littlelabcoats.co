🌦️ Weather All Around Us

Wind  ·  Rain  ·  Temperature  ·  Seasons
💨
WIND
  • Moving air
  • Moves clouds + seeds
  • 🌳 Bends trees
  • We feel it not see it
🌧️
RAIN
  • Water from clouds
  • Fills rivers + lakes
  • Part of water cycle
  • 💧 Evaporates back up
🌡️
TEMPERATURE
  • How hot or cold
  • ☀️ Summer = hottest
  • ❄️ Winter = coldest
  • Thermometer measures it
🍂
SEASONS
  • Earth tilts as it orbits
  • 🌸 Spring: warming + growing
  • ☀️ Summer: longest days
  • ❄️ Winter: shortest days
⚠️
No Wind
Seeds can't spread
⚠️
No Rain
Plants and animals die
⚠️
No Seasons
Same weather always
⚠️
No Temperature
Can't dress right!
🌩️ Lightning heats air to 30,000°F
🌀 Hurricanes can be 300 miles wide
❄️ Snowflakes are always 6-sided
