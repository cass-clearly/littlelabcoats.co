🌿 Garden Detectives Reference Card

One-page unit reference card for quick parent coaching.

1. Site clues

Soil, water, and sunlight decide what can thrive.

2. Leaf clues

Shape, veins, edges, and texture are evidence.

3. Part jobs

Roots, stems, leaves, flowers, and seeds work as a team.

4. Belonging decisions

Weeds are plants in conflict with a goal, not villains.

5. Planning choices

Annuals, perennials, space, sun, and care are design constraints.

6. Pollinator invitation

Flower traits and safe habitat invite garden helpers.

Parent coaching sentence: “What did you notice, what evidence supports that, and how should that change your garden plan?”
