✍️ CER Short Response

Prompt: Which condition helped mold grow the most in your investigation? Write a claim, include at least two pieces of evidence, and explain your reasoning.

👩‍🏫 Parent/Teacher Notes

  • Push past vague answers: “It got moldy” is not enough evidence. Students should compare timing, amount, or visible differences between setups.
  • Look for variable thinking: Strong answers identify what changed and what stayed the same.
  • Best outcome: Students see mold as part of decomposition, not just as something gross.

Detach note: This page is for parent/teacher reference. Student wording may vary if the science reasoning is correct.

Short Response Guidance

  • Student clearly names the condition they think helped mold most.
  • Student includes specific observations (day numbers, amount of fuzz, color, spread, etc.).
  • Student connects evidence to fungal growth conditions and fair testing.
