What Success Looks Like

  • Bread mold is a fungus
  • Mold is a decomposer
  • Keeping the bags sealed is a safety step
  • Core science takeaway: Student explains that decomposition recycles nutrients instead of treating mold as only a safety problem or gross surprise.

Short Response Guidance

  • Student states a correct science idea from the lesson.
  • Student uses one real observation, comparison, or test result as evidence.
  • Student connects the evidence to the idea instead of only restating a vocabulary word.
  • Look for decomposition reasoning such as “the mold grew fastest in the damp bag, showing fungi break down dead material when conditions help them grow.”
