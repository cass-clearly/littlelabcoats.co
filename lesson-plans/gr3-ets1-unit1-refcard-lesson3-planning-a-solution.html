🛠️ Planning a Solution

Grade 3 Engineering — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Engineers compare more than one possible solution before choosing what to build.

Key Vocabulary
  • solution: an idea for solving the problem
  • sketch: a drawing that shows the design idea
  • tradeoff: a choice with both a benefit and a cost
  • compare: to look at how two ideas are alike or different
  • label: a note that explains a part of the design
Remember This
  • Two sketches are better than one rushed idea.
  • Engineers compare plans using the criteria and constraints.
  • A good sketch shows where padding, support, and object placement will happen.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: I compared Idea A and Idea B...
  • Add: I chose this plan because...
  • Finish with: It best fits the criteria because...
