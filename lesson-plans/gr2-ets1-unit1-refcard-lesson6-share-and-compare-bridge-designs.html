🌉 Share and Compare Bridge Designs

Grade 2 Engineering — Bridge Builders Quick Reference
Big Idea

A final engineering share-out should include the problem, the final bridge, the key improvement, and the evidence that supports it.

Key Vocabulary
  • final design: the improved version you share
  • improvement: a change that helped
  • evidence: what the test showed
  • compare: show how version 2 changed from version 1
  • success: how the bridge met the goal
Remember This
  • Say what problem the bridge solved before you describe the design.
  • Name the key improvement from version 1 to version 2.
  • Use test evidence to explain why the final bridge is stronger.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: My bridge needs to...
  • Add: I chose this shape because...
  • Finish with: The evidence showed...
