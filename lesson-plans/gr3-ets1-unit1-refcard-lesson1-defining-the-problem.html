🛠️ Defining the Problem

Grade 3 Engineering — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Before engineers build, they define the need, the user, and what success must look like.

Key Vocabulary
  • problem: something that needs to be solved
  • user: the person or thing the design must help
  • criteria: what success looks like
  • fragile: easy to break or damage
  • need: something important that must be solved
Remember This
  • The challenge in this unit is to protect a fragile object during a drop.
  • A strong problem statement says who or what needs help and what the design must do.
  • If the problem is unclear, the rest of the design process becomes random.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: The problem is...
  • Add: The design needs to...
  • Finish with: Success means...
