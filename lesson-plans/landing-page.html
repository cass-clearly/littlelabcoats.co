About the founder

Hi, I'm Serena. 👋

I'm a scientist. I have a first-class degree in Zoology, a Master's from Oxford in Integrative Bioscience, and a PhD in Microbiology. I've spent my career doing real science — asking questions, running experiments, testing ideas.

I'm also a homeschool mom of three. And when I went looking for science curriculum that was actually good — rigorous, hands-on, beautiful, and built for families with kids at different ages — I couldn't find it.

So I built it myself.

Little Lab Coats is everything I wished existed when I started homeschooling. Real experiments. Multi-age lessons. Beautiful materials my kids actually want to use. And nothing that requires a science degree to teach — because I've written the guides so any parent can feel confident in the material.

Science changed how I see the world. I want to give that to your kids too.
