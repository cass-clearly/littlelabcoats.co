Trait Data Detective Reference Card

Grade 3 · Life Science
Scientists use data tables and graphs to support claims about inherited traits and variation.
Key vocabulary
datagraphclaimsupport
What to notice
  • Data turns observations into evidence.
  • Graphs make patterns easier to see.
  • Accurate wording matters in scientific claims.
Materials snapshot
  • Class trait chart or printed data set, worksheet, pencil, crayons
Talk-about prompts
  • What did the graph show most clearly?
  • Where did you see variation?
  • How does data strengthen a claim?
Why it matters

This unit culminates in actual evidence interpretation, matching the NGSS expectation more directly.
