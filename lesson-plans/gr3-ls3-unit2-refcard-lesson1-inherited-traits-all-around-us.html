Inherited Traits All Around Us Reference Card

Grade 3 · Life Science
Traits are features of living things, and many traits are passed from parents to offspring.
Key vocabulary
traitinheritoffspringparent
What to notice
  • Shared traits can be observed directly.
  • Parent-offspring comparisons provide evidence.
  • Traits can appear in both plants and animals.
Materials snapshot
  • Family pet or animal pictures, plant pictures, worksheet, pencil
Talk-about prompts
  • Which trait appeared in both the parent and offspring?
  • What makes this evidence stronger?
  • Why are similarities not the whole story?
Why it matters

This lesson helps children see inheritance as an evidence-based pattern, not just a guess.
