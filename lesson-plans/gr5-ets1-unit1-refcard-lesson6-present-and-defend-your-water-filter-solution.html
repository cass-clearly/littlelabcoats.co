🏁 Present and Defend Your Water Filter Solution

Grade 5 Engineering — Clean Water Design Lab Quick Reference
Big Idea

A strong engineering explanation connects the problem, redesign, evidence, and trade-offs.

Key Vocabulary
  • claim: what you say about your design
  • evidence: the data or observations that support the claim
  • reasoning: why the evidence matters
  • trade-off: a remaining downside or compromise
Remember This
  • Compare version 1 and version 2 directly.
  • Use criteria and evidence to defend the final design.
  • Honest engineers name limits as well as strengths.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: Our problem was...
  • Add: Version 2 improved because...
  • Finish with: The trade-off is..., but our design is still strong because...
