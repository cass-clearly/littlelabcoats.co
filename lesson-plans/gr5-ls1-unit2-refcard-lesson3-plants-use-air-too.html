Plants Use Air Too Reference Card

Grade 5 · Life Science
Plants do not build themselves from soil alone; they also use gases from the air.
Key vocabulary
carbon dioxideairleafgas
What to notice
  • Air provides a material input.
  • Invisible processes still require evidence and models.
  • Leaves are the main entry point in this explanation.
Materials snapshot
  • Leaf diagram or picture, worksheet, pencil
Talk-about prompts
  • What does the leaf take in from the air?
  • Why is this idea easy to miss?
  • How did the model help?
Why it matters

This lesson closes the air side of the standard directly and explicitly.
