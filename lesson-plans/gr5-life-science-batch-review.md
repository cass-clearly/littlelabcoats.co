## Rachel review artifact

Status: `round 1 commented — revisions applied`
Round: `1`
Scope label: `Grade 5 LS1 Unit 2 — Plant Matter & Growth`

### Rachel round-1 review notes

1. **Target:** `gr5-ls1-unit2-lesson1-what-do-plants-need-to-grow.html`
   **Observation:** The first lesson correctly introduces the misconception, but the worksheet ends with a very open “first claim” prompt that does not force students to explain why air and water count as material inputs while sunlight and soil play different roles.
   **Expected revision action:** Add explicit worksheet coaching and tighten the answer key so the role distinctions are named directly.

2. **Target:** `gr5-ls1-unit2-lesson2-water-moves-into-plants.html`
   **Observation:** The colored-water activity is strong, but the evidence statement prompt is too loose. Students could write a vague sentence without naming where the color appeared or what that proves about water entering the plant.
   **Expected revision action:** Tighten the worksheet prompt and answer key so observation location + movement + evidence claim are all required.

3. **Target:** `gr5-ls1-unit2-quiz.html`
   **Observation:** The multiple-choice “answer key” currently reads like lesson-objective summaries instead of concrete content answers, which weakens parent grading support.
   **Expected revision action:** Replace the list with real answer-key language and make the short-response grading cues require specific evidence categories.
