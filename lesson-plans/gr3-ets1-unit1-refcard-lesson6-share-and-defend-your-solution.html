🛠️ Share and Defend Your Solution

Grade 3 Engineering — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

A strong engineering defense connects the problem, the design, and the evidence.

Key Vocabulary
  • defend: to support an idea with reasons and evidence
  • criteria: what the design needed to do
  • constraint: a limit the design had to follow
  • evidence: observations or test results that support a claim
  • redesign: a revised version made after testing
Remember This
  • Engineers explain why their solution should be trusted.
  • A final defense should mention the problem, criteria, constraints, testing, and redesign.
  • The best defenses sound specific, not vague.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: My solution is strong because...
  • Add: My evidence is...
  • Finish with: This shows my design met the challenge because...
