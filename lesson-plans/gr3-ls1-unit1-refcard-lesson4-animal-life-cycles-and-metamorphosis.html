🐸 Animal Life Cycles and Metamorphosis

Grade 3 Life Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Some animals change gradually and some change dramatically, but both kinds of life cycles still follow the same shared pattern.

Key Vocabulary
  • Metamorphosis: a major body change between stages
  • Larva: an early stage in some animal life cycles
  • Adult: the mature stage that can reproduce
  • Offspring: young organisms made through reproduction
Remember This
  • A butterfly and a frog go through metamorphosis.
  • A bird or human grows more gradually.
  • All of these life cycles still include birth, growth, reproduction, and death.
Use It to Explain
  • Use one clue from the stages to explain whether the animal changes gradually or dramatically.
  • Name where reproduction appears: the adult stage.
  • Remind students that metamorphosis is still the same organism changing over time.
