🧠 Model and Explain a Life Cycle

Grade 3 Life Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

A strong scientific model is clear, labeled, and useful for explaining evidence about birth, growth, reproduction, and death.

Key Vocabulary
  • Model: a drawing or diagram that helps explain science
  • Claim: an idea you say is true
  • Evidence: details from your model that support the claim
  • Stage: one step in the life cycle
Remember This
  • Label the stages clearly and put them in order.
  • Show where reproduction happens.
  • Use the model as evidence, not just as a neat picture.
Use It to Explain
  • Check for labels, arrows, and stage order.
  • Make sure the explanation names birth, growth, reproduction, and death.
  • Revise the model if the science is clear in your head but not yet clear on the page.
