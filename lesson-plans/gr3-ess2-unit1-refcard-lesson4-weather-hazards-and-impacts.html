⛈️ Weather Hazards and Impacts

Grade 3 Earth Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

A weather event becomes a hazard when it can cause harm or dangerous conditions.

Key Vocabulary
  • Hazard: a condition that can cause harm
  • Impact: what happens because of the hazard
  • Flood: water covering land that is usually dry
  • Warning: an alert that helps people prepare
Remember This
  • A hazard matters because of its impact on people, places, or living things.
  • Different hazards cause different kinds of problems in a community.
  • Cause/effect language helps explain why a hazard matters.
