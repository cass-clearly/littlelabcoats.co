Tracking Change Through One Life Cycle Reference Card

Grade 3 · Life Science
Follow one organism through time first. Reproduction is the point where a new generation begins.
Key vocabulary
timelinestagegenerationoffspring
Remember this
  • The caterpillar, chrysalis, and butterfly are the same organism at different times.
  • Reproduction begins a new organism; it is not just “more growth.”
  • Ask: “Same organism or new generation?”
Use it to explain
  • Point to the stage where the same organism is changing.
  • Name the moment when a new generation begins.
  • Use the words reproduction and offspring correctly.
Family reminder: If your child gets confused, use one counter as the “offspring token” and place it only where the new generation starts.
