📋 NGSS Alignment:
K-2-ETS1-1 / K-2-ETS1-3 — Ask questions, define a simple problem, and compare possible solutions.

🎯 Learning Objective

Students will define a desk problem, build a quick pencil holder model, test it against criteria, and improve the design.

💡 The Big Idea

A good engineering solution matches the problem, criteria, and evidence from tests.

🔬 Key Vocabulary

problemcriteriasolutionmodeltestredesign

📦 Materials

  • Index cards or scrap paper
  • Tape
  • 3 pencils or crayons
  • Small cup optional
  • Ruler optional
  • Worksheet

🧪 Investigation

Step 1 — Define the problem
Pencils keep rolling away from the work area.
Step 2 — Set criteria
The holder should keep three pencils in place and stand on its own.
Step 3 — Build version 1
Fold/tape paper or use a cup idea to make a holder.
Step 4 — Test
Place three pencils and gently bump the table.
Step 5 — Improve
Change the base, height, or shape.
Step 6 — Compare solutions
Student names which version worked better and why.
