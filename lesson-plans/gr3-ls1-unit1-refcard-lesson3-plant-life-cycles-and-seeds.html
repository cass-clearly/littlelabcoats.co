🌱 Plant Life Cycles and Seeds

Grade 3 Life Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Plants have unique life cycles, but they still show birth, growth, reproduction, and death.

Key Vocabulary
  • Seed: the beginning stage of a new plant
  • Seedling: a young plant that has started to grow
  • Reproduce: make new seeds that can become new plants
  • Life cycle: the pattern of birth, growth, reproduction, and death
Remember This
  • A seed is part of the plant’s birth stage.
  • A mature plant reproduces by making new seeds.
  • The stages may look different from animal stages, but the same big pattern still appears.
Use It to Explain
  • Name where birth appears: the seed.
  • Name where growth appears: the seedling and mature plant.
  • Name where reproduction appears: the plant makes new seeds.
