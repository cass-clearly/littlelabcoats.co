📊 Reading Weather Data

Grade 3 Earth Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Charts and graphs make weather evidence easier to compare and explain.

Key Vocabulary
  • Data: information collected from observations
  • Chart: an organized display of information
  • Graph: a visual way to compare information
  • Trend: the direction a pattern seems to move
Remember This
  • Read the title and labels before deciding what the chart means.
  • A strong answer points to a specific bar, number, or symbol as evidence.
  • Charts help scientists compare days more clearly than memory alone.
