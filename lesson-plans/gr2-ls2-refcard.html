🌿 Living Things Need Each Other

Ecosystems  ·  Food Chains  ·  Habitats  ·  Pollinators
🌍
ECOSYSTEMS
  • Living + nonliving together
  • Plants, animals, soil, water
  • All parts are connected
  • Change one → change all
⛓️
FOOD CHAINS
  • Energy flows from the sun
  • Sun → Plant → Herbivore → Carnivore
  • Arrows show energy direction
  • Remove one link → chain breaks
🐝
POLLINATORS
  • Move pollen between flowers
  • 🐝 Bees   🦋 Butterflies   🐦 Hummingbirds
  • Without them: no fruit or seeds
  • 1 in 3 bites of food needs pollinators
🏡
HABITATS
  • Where an animal lives
  • Must have: food, water, shelter, space
  • 🐟 Fish in water   🦅 Eagles in mountains
  • Destroy habitat → lose the animal
☀️
No Sun
No plants, no food chain
🐝
No Pollinators
No fruit or seeds
🪱
No Decomposers
Dead things pile up
💧
No Clean Water
Ecosystem collapses
🐛 One earthworm can eat half its body weight in soil daily
🌻 A single oak tree supports 500+ species
🦋 Monarch butterflies migrate 3,000 miles
🐝 Bees can recognize human faces
