Movement Traits in Different Places Reference Card

Grade 3 · Life Science
A movement trait can be an advantage when it fits the terrain or challenge of the environment.
Key vocabulary
movementterrainstreamlinedwebbed
What to notice
  • Movement traits are environment-specific advantages.
  • Comparisons help clarify which traits are helpful.
  • A good explanation names both trait and setting.
Materials snapshot
  • Movement picture cards, worksheet, pencil
Talk-about prompts
  • What made this trait useful here?
  • What trait would struggle in this setting?
  • How did comparison strengthen your claim?
Why it matters

Movement provides another clean example of trait variation linked to survival advantage.

Try this next: Watch birds, ducks, or squirrels outside and infer which movement traits help them most.
