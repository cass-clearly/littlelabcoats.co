🧪 Compare Possible Filter Materials and Systems

Grade 5 Engineering — Clean Water Design Lab Quick Reference
Big Idea

Different filter materials and layer orders create different strengths, weaknesses, and trade-offs.

Key Vocabulary
  • flow rate: how quickly water moves through the system
  • layer: one material level in a design
  • particle: a tiny piece of matter in the dirty water
  • system: parts working together to do a job
Remember This
  • Layer order can change how well a design works.
  • A material that traps more may also slow the flow.
  • Comparing ideas first leads to smarter testing.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: This material might help because...
  • Add: A possible trade-off is...
  • Finish with: We should test this idea first because...
