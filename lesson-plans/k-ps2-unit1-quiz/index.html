🚀 Forces & Motion Quiz

Unit 1 — Physical Science • Kindergarten

📖 Ask a grown-up to read the questions to you!
⭐ Score: 0 / 10
👐 1 When you push something, which way does it move?
🔵 A push moves something away from you! When you push a toy car, it rolls away. A pull is different — that moves something toward you. Remember: push = away, pull = toward!
🧲 2 What do we call it when a magnet pulls something toward it?
🧲 When a magnet pulls something toward it, we say it attracts it! Magnets attract iron and steel — like paperclips and metal spoons. If two magnets push each other away, that's called repel.
⛷️ 3 When a ball rolls down a ramp, what force pulls it downward?
🌍 Gravity is the force that pulls everything downward toward the Earth. When you put a ball at the top of a ramp, gravity pulls it down and it rolls! Without gravity, balls wouldn't roll anywhere.
💪 4 If you give a ball a STRONG push, what happens compared to a gentle push?
💪 A strong push makes things go farther and faster than a gentle push! The bigger the force, the more the object moves. That's why throwing a ball hard sends it much farther than a tiny tap.
🪝 5 Opening a drawer is an example of what kind of force?
🪝 Opening a drawer is a pull! You grab the handle and pull it toward you. A pull moves things toward you. Closing a drawer is a push — you push it away from you!
🎢 6 What happens to a marble on a ramp when the ramp is made STEEPER?
🎢 A steeper ramp makes a marble roll faster! The steeper the angle, the more gravity pulls it down quickly. That's why slides at the playground that are very steep are more fun and fast!
💨 7 Wind is moving air. What does wind DO to things in its path?
💨 Wind pushes things! When air moves fast and hits something — like a sail or a leaf — it pushes it. That's how sailboats move and how kites fly! Engineers use wind's push to make wind wagons and windmills.
📎 8 Which of these things would a magnet attract (pick up)?
📎 Magnets attract metal paperclips! Magnets can pull iron and steel toward them. Wood, plastic, and paper are not magnetic — a magnet won't pick those up. Only certain metals work with magnets!
🛠️ 9 An engineer builds a wind wagon but it doesn't move far. What should she do NEXT?
🛠️ Engineers always improve their designs! The Engineering Design Process says: Ask, Imagine, Plan, Create, Test — and then Improve! When something doesn't work perfectly, you figure out why and try again. That's how great inventions are made!
🔴 10 Two magnets push each other AWAY. What word describes this?
🧲 When two magnets push each other away, we say they repel! Opposite ends of a magnet (north and south) attract each other. But the same ends (north-north or south-south) repel — they push away from each other!
