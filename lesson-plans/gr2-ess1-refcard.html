🌍 Earth Is Always Changing

Landforms  ·  Rocks  ·  Erosion  ·  Weather
🏔️
LANDFORMS
  • Shapes on Earth's surface
  • Mountains, valleys, plains, islands
  • Made by volcanoes and movement
  • Change very slowly over time
🪨
ROCKS & SOIL
  • Made of minerals
  • Igneous, sedimentary, metamorphic
  • Soil = tiny rock bits + organic matter
  • Darker soil = richer in nutrients
💧
EROSION
  • Water and wind move rock + soil
  • Rivers carve canyons
  • Rain washes hillsides
  • Can take millions of years
🌦️
WEATHER
  • Short-term sky conditions
  • Sun, rain, wind, snow
  • Measured daily
  • Climate = patterns over years
⚠️
No Soil
Can't grow food
⚠️
No Rain
Erosion still happens (wind!)
⚠️
No Landforms
Earth is flat everywhere
⚠️
No Weather Patterns
Can't predict or prepare
🏔️ The Grand Canyon was carved by water over 5-6 million years
🪨 The oldest rocks on Earth are 4 billion years old
🌊 Ocean waves move 500 lbs of sand per wave
🌧️ The wettest place on Earth gets 467 inches of rain per year
