Key vocabulary
animalneedfoodwaterairshelter
What to notice
  • Animals need food, water, air, and shelter.
  • Different animals meet needs in different ways.
  • A habitat checkup makes survival needs visible.
Materials snapshot
  • Toy animal or animal picture
  • Small box, towel, or paper “home”
  • Blue paper or cup for water symbol
  • Pretend food card
  • Crayons
  • Checklist worksheet
Talk-about prompts
  • Which need did you add first?
  • What would happen if water were missing?
  • How is this animal’s home different from yours?
Why it matters

A strong answer includes food, water, air, and shelter and explains that missing a need can make the animal unsafe or unable to survive.

Family bridge: This card helps parents coach the evidence sentence with quick, concrete language at home.
