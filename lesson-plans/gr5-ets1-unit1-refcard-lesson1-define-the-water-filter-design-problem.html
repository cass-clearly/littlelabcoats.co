💧 Define the Water Filter Design Problem

Grade 5 Engineering — Clean Water Design Lab Quick Reference
Big Idea

Before building, engineers define measurable success criteria and realistic constraints for the prototype.

Key Vocabulary
  • criteria: the specific ways the design must succeed
  • constraint: a limit the design must follow
  • prototype: a first build used for testing
  • trade-off: when helping one feature can weaken another
  • design brief: a short summary of the engineering problem
Remember This
  • The challenge is about model dirty water, not drinking water.
  • Strong design briefs name the need, criteria, and constraints.
  • Good engineers think ahead about how success will be measured.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: Our design must...
  • Add: One important constraint is...
  • Finish with: We will know it worked if...
