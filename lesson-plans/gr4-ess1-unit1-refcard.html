🌍 Shaping Earth’s Surface

Weathering · Erosion · Deposition · Evidence
🪨
WEATHERING
  • Breaks rock into smaller pieces
  • Water, roots, rubbing, ice, and temperature can help
  • Makes the sediment erosion can later move
  • Often happens slowly but constantly
💧
EROSION
  • Moves sediment from place to place
  • Water, wind, ice, and gravity can all transport material
  • Channels, cut banks, and bare soil are clues
  • Vegetation can slow the rate
🏞️
DEPOSITION
  • Happens when moving water or wind slows down
  • Larger particles usually settle first
  • Builds deltas, sandbars, beaches, and layers
  • Adds new material to Earth’s surface
🗺️
EVIDENCE & PATTERNS
  • Maps and models help us compare change over time
  • Measurements make explanations stronger
  • Scientists match surface clues to likely processes
  • Claim + evidence + reasoning = stronger science
💧
Water
Cuts channels and carries sediment
🌬️
Wind
Moves loose particles across open ground
🧊
Ice
Scrapes and pushes material
🌱
Plants
Help hold soil in place
