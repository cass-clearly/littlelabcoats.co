🔧 Engineers Solve Problems

Ask  ·  Imagine  ·  Plan  ·  Build  ·  Test  ·  Improve
🔍
FIND THE PROBLEM
  • What isn't working?
  • Ask: Who needs help?
  • 🌂 Umbrella: stay dry
  • Every invention solves something
✏️
PLAN & DESIGN
  • Sketch your idea
  • Label the parts
  • Think: what materials?
  • Blueprint = plan on paper
🏗️
BUILD & TEST
  • Make your design
  • Does it work?
  • Test = find what fails
  • Failure = learning!
🔄
IMPROVE
  • Change what didn't work
  • Test again
  • Keep improving
  • Best designs take many tries
