Variation Within a Similar Group Reference Card

Grade 3 · Life Science
Organisms in the same kind of group can share many traits and still show variation.
Key vocabulary
variationsimilargrouppattern
What to notice
  • Variation can be measured or tallied.
  • Similarity and difference can both be true.
  • Groups provide stronger evidence than single examples.
Materials snapshot
  • Picture set of similar organisms, worksheet, pencil
Talk-about prompts
  • Which differences counted as variation?
  • What traits were shared by most of the group?
  • How did the tally help?
Why it matters

Variation is a core bridge to later survival and adaptation thinking.
