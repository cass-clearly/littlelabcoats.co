Grade 2 Engineering — Bridge Builders Quick Reference
Big Idea

A plan should show the bridge shape, label the important parts, and connect each part to a job.

Key Vocabulary
  • plan: your sketch before building
  • label: name a part
  • model: a smaller version to test
  • support: part that helps hold it up
  • evidence: what you learned from testing
Remember This
  • Start with the shape that worked best in your fair test.
  • Label the parts that help the bridge stay strong.
  • A bridge plan should be easy for another person to understand.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: My bridge needs to...
  • Add: I chose this shape because...
  • Finish with: The evidence showed...
