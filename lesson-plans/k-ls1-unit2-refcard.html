🌱 From Seed to Plant

Seeds  ·  Roots  ·  Stems  ·  Leaves  ·  Flowers
🌰
SEEDS
  • Baby plant inside
  • Need water + warmth
  • Coat protects embryo
  • Germination = sprouting
🪱
ROOTS
  • Drink up water
  • Anchor plant in soil
  • 🥕 Carrots are roots
  • Grow downward
🍃
LEAVES
  • Catch sunlight
  • Make food for plant
  • 🌿 Green = chlorophyll
  • Turn yellow without sun
🌸
FLOWERS
  • Make new seeds
  • Attract pollinators
  • 🐝 Bees carry pollen
  • Fruit holds seeds
⚠️
No Water
Seeds won't germinate
⚠️
No Sunlight
Leaves turn pale
⚠️
No Roots
Plant tips over
⚠️
No Flowers
No new seeds
🌻 Sunflowers face the sun all day
🥥 Coconuts are seeds that float
🌵 Cacti store water in their stems
🍓 Strawberry seeds are on the outside!
