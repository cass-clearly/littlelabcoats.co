🌦️ Weather Patterns Over Time

Grade 3 Earth Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Weather records help scientists notice short-term patterns across several days.

Key Vocabulary
  • Weather: what the air and sky are doing right now
  • Observation: a detail you notice and record
  • Pattern: something that repeats in a useful way
  • Precipitation: rain, snow, sleet, or hail
Remember This
  • One day of weather is an observation. Several days can show a pattern.
  • Use the weather record itself as evidence when you explain a pattern.
  • A pattern can include changes that repeat, not only things that stay exactly the same.
