## Grade 5 explicit strand check (Serena-named)

**Overall judgment:** Grade 5 is **not complete**.

- **Inheritance and traits — missing**
  - Current state: Grade 5 life science contains ecosystems (`gr5-ls1-unit1-*`) and plant matter/growth (`gr5-ls1-unit2-*`).
  - Missing need: no Grade 5 heredity/trait variation lane that builds past Grade 3 inheritance work.
  - Planned content need: a full upper-elementary heredity/traits unit with evidence, variation, and explanation/argument work.

- **Space content — missing**
  - Current state: no canonical Grade 5 space unit in `lesson-plans/`.
  - Missing need: upper-elementary Earth/space follow-through beyond Grade 1 sky-pattern content.
  - Planned content need: a canonical Grade 5 space/solar-system lane, not just isolated references.

- **Atoms work — missing**
  - Current state: `gr5-ps1-lesson1-reactants-products-mixtures.html` exists as a single chemistry lesson plus refcard/quiz, but there is no full matter/particle/atoms progression.
  - Missing need: Grade 5 physical science does not yet deliver a usable particle-model / atoms / matter-structure lane.
  - Planned content need: a real multi-lesson PS1 unit, not a one-lesson chemistry stub.

- **Fossils — incomplete**
  - Current state: `lesson-plans/5th-fossils-earth-history.html` exists, but only as a standalone legacy-style lesson.
  - Missing need: fossils are not integrated into a canonical Grade 5 earth-science unit structure with supporting progression.
  - Planned content need: either a full fossils / Earth-history unit or a clear canonical Earth-history sequence where fossils are a real strand, not a one-off.

- **Weather — missing**
  - Current state: Grade 5 earth science centers on water systems (`gr5-ess1-unit1-*`); no canonical Grade 5 weather/climate lane exists.
  - Missing need: no upper-grade weather follow-through after strong K–3 weather coverage.
  - Planned content need: a Grade 5 weather / climate / atmosphere lane with more advanced data and system reasoning.
