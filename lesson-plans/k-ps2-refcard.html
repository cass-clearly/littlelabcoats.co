⚡ Forces Make Things Move

Push  ·  Pull  ·  Gravity  ·  Friction
👐
PUSH
  • Moves things away
  • 🏀 Throw a ball
  • 🚪 Open a door
  • Gets harder with distance
🧲
PULL
  • Moves things closer
  • 🎣 Reel in fish
  • 🪁 Pull a sled
  • Magnets pull metal
🌍
GRAVITY
  • Pulls everything down
  • 🍎 Apple falls from tree
  • 🌧️ Rain falls down
  • Can't turn it off!
🛞
FRICTION
  • Slows things down
  • 🛷 Rough road = slow
  • 🧊 Ice = less friction
  • Makes heat!
⚠️
No Push
Things stay still
⚠️
No Pull
Can't bring things closer
⚠️
No Gravity
Everything floats away
⚠️
No Friction
Nothing would stop
🌙 Moon has 1/6 Earth's gravity
🧊 Ice is slippery — very low friction
🧲 Magnets push AND pull
🏋️ Heavier = more gravity pull
