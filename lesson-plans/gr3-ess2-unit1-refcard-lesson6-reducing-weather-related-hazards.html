🛠️ Reducing Weather-Related Hazards

Grade 3 Earth Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

A design solution has merit when it clearly reduces the impact of a weather-related hazard.

Key Vocabulary
  • Merit: being worth choosing because it fits the problem well
  • Claim: a statement you defend with reasons
  • Evidence: details that support the claim
  • Reduce: to make something smaller or less harmful
Remember This
  • A merit claim should name the hazard, the solution, and why the solution works.
  • Evidence makes a design claim stronger than opinion alone.
  • Revision helps you notice when a claim still needs clearer reasons or evidence.
