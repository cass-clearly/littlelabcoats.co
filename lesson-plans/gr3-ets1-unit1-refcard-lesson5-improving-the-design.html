🛠️ Improving the Design

Grade 3 Engineering — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Engineers redesign by changing the part that evidence shows needs improvement.

Key Vocabulary
  • improve: to make something work better
  • revise: to change using new information
  • version: one form of a design
  • evidence: information that supports a decision
  • predict: to explain what should happen next and why
Remember This
  • Version 2 should respond to version 1 evidence.
  • Targeted redesign is stronger than changing everything at once.
  • A redesign claim is strongest when it names the change and why it should help.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: I changed...
  • Add: I made that change because...
  • Finish with: Version 2 should work better because...
