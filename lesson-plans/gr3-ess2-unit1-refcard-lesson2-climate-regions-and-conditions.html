🌍 Climate Regions and Conditions

Grade 3 Earth Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Climate describes the usual weather pattern in a place over a long time.

Key Vocabulary
  • Climate: the usual weather pattern in a region over time
  • Region: a place or area with shared conditions
  • Usual: what happens most of the time
  • Long-term: happening across a long period, not just one day
Remember This
  • Weather is right now. Climate is the usual pattern over time.
  • Two places can have different climates even though both have daily weather.
  • One hot or rainy day does not tell you the whole climate of a region.
