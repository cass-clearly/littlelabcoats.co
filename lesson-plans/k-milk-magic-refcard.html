Milk Magic Reference Card

Grade K
Soap breaks surface tension and grabs fat, so the food coloring races across the milk.
Key vocabulary
surface tensionfatobservationvariable
What to notice
  • Whole milk usually creates the strongest movement.
  • Soap breaks the invisible skin on top of the milk.
  • The first soap touch is the most dramatic.
  • Changing one thing at a time helps you test fairly.
Materials snapshot
  • Whole milk
  • Skim milk
  • Food coloring
  • Dish soap
  • Cotton swabs or toothpicks
Talk-about prompts
  • What changed first when the soap touched the milk?
  • Which plate made the bigger color burst?
  • What stayed the same in both tests?
  • What did you change between the two plates?
Why it matters

This experiment gives Kindergarten students a fast, joyful way to notice cause and effect. They can see that changing the kind of milk changes the size of the swirling color burst.

Try this next: Try 2% milk or cream and rank which liquid makes the biggest color burst.
