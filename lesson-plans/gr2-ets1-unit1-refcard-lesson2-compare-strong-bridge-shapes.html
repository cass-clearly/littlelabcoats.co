🌉 Compare Strong Bridge Shapes

Grade 2 Engineering — Bridge Builders Quick Reference
Big Idea

The same material can act differently when its shape changes. Engineers compare shapes before they choose one.

Key Vocabulary
  • span: reach across a gap
  • sag: bend downward
  • support: what holds it up
  • compare: check two choices side by side
  • strength: how well it holds up
Remember This
  • A fair test uses the same gap and the same type of weight each time.
  • Folded or ridged paper often stays stronger than flat paper.
  • The best bridge idea is the one supported by your test evidence.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: My bridge needs to...
  • Add: I chose this shape because...
  • Finish with: The evidence showed...
