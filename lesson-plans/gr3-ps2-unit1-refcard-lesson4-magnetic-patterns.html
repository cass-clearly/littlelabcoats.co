🧲 Magnetic Patterns

Grade 3 Physical Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Testing many objects helps us find a pattern about what magnets usually attract.

Key Vocabulary
  • Pattern: something that happens again and again
  • Evidence: observations from tests
  • Predict: use a pattern to make a good guess
Remember This
  • Many iron or steel objects are magnetic.
  • Many wood, plastic, paper, and fabric objects are not magnetic.
  • Patterns help scientists explain and predict.
