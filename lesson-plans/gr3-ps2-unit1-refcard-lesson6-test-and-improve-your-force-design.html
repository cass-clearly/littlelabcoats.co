🛠️ Test and Improve Your Force Design

Grade 3 Physical Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Engineers improve designs by testing them and using evidence to make better versions.

Key Vocabulary
  • Redesign: change a design to improve it
  • Evidence: what happened during testing
  • Compare: decide how version 1 and version 2 are alike or different
Remember This
  • Testing tells you what needs to change.
  • Version 2 should connect to evidence from version 1.
  • Improving a design is part of engineering success.
