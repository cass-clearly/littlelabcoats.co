🛠️ Researching Constraints and Materials

Grade 3 Engineering — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Engineers study limits and material properties before they decide how to build.

Key Vocabulary
  • constraint: a rule or limit the design must follow
  • material: what the design is made from
  • property: a quality such as soft, stiff, or flexible
  • cushion: to soften an impact
  • support: to help hold shape or weight
Remember This
  • Constraints can include limited materials, size limits, or time limits.
  • Different materials do different jobs. Soft materials cushion; stiff materials support.
  • Good engineering choices connect a material property to the problem.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: A key constraint is...
  • Add: This material helps because...
  • Finish with: That matters for the design because...
