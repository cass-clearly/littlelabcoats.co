⚖️ Balanced and Unbalanced Forces

Grade 3 Physical Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Balanced forces cancel out. Unbalanced forces cause motion to change.

Key Vocabulary
  • Force: a push or a pull
  • Balanced: equal forces that cancel out
  • Unbalanced: one force is stronger
  • Motion: movement
  • Evidence: what you observed in a test
Remember This
  • Balanced does not mean “no forces.” It means the forces match.
  • If one force wins, the object's motion changes.
  • Use what you observed as evidence when you explain your answer.
