⚖️ Balanced and Unbalanced Forces

Grade 3 Physical Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

A force is a push or a pull. Balanced forces cancel out. Unbalanced forces cause motion to change.

Key Vocabulary
  • Force: a push or a pull
  • Balanced forces: equal forces that cancel out
  • Unbalanced forces: unequal forces that change motion
  • Motion: movement
  • Evidence: what you observed in a test
Examples
  • If two equal pushes act on opposite sides of an object, it may stay still.
  • If one push is stronger than the other, the object may move.
What to Remember
  • Balanced does not mean “no forces.” It means the forces match.
  • Unbalanced means one force wins.
  • Use observations as evidence when you explain motion.
