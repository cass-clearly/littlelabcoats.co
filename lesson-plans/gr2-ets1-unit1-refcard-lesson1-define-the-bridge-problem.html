🌉 Define the Bridge Problem

Grade 2 Engineering — Bridge Builders Quick Reference
Big Idea

Before building, engineers decide who needs the bridge, what gap it must cross, and what success means.

Key Vocabulary
  • bridge: a structure that crosses a gap
  • gap: the space to cross
  • criteria: the success rules
  • constraint: a limit you must follow
  • user: who the bridge helps
Remember This
  • The bridge challenge is for a toy animal crossing a gap.
  • A strong problem statement names the user, the gap, and the success goal.
  • If the problem is clear, the plan gets easier.
Use It to Explain
  • Start with: My bridge needs to...
  • Add: I chose this shape because...
  • Finish with: The evidence showed...
