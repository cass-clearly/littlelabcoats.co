🔊 Sound & Light Travel Everywhere

Vibration  ·  Pitch  ·  Volume  ·  Reflection
🔊
SOUND
  • Made by vibrations
  • Stop vibration = silence
  • 🥁 Drum vibrates when hit
  • Travels through air
🎵
PITCH
  • High or low sound
  • Thin string = high pitch
  • Thick string = low pitch
  • 🐦 Birds = high / 🐻 Bears = low
💡
LIGHT
  • Travels in straight lines
  • Bounces off mirrors
  • Can't go through solid
  • 🌈 White light = all colors
📢
VOLUME
  • Loud or soft
  • Harder hit = louder
  • Far away = quieter
  • 🌊 Waves carry sound energy
⚠️
No Vibration
No sound at all
⚠️
No Light
Complete darkness
⚠️
No Reflection
No mirrors work
⚠️
No Pitch
All sounds the same
🌙 Sound can't travel in space (no air!)
🐬 Dolphins use sound to "see" (echolocation)
💡 Light travels 186,000 miles per second
🦇 Bats navigate in total darkness using sound
