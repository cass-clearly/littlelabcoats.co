👂 Audiobook + Pause 15 min

Listening Comprehension

Time to use your super ears! 🦻 You'll listen to part of an audiobook, then hit pause and answer some questions about what you heard.

  1. Pick an audiobook (any story you like!).
  2. Listen for 5–10 minutes. Really focus!
  3. Hit PAUSE ⏸️ and answer the questions on your worksheet.
  4. If you want, listen to another chunk and answer again!
💡 Listening Tip: Close your eyes while you listen. It helps you picture what's happening in the story!

🔤 Root Word Detective 10 min

Word Analysis — Prefixes

Today you're a Word Detective! 🕵️ You'll learn a prefix and use it to crack the meaning of new words.

🔑 Today's Prefix: UN-

"un-" means "not" or "the opposite of."

Examples:

  • un + happy = unhappy → not happy 😢
  • un + lock = unlock → the opposite of lock 🔓
  • un + kind = unkind → not kind 😠
  1. Look at the prefix "un-" above.
  2. On your worksheet, find 5 words that start with "un-".
  3. Guess what each word means FIRST — then check!
💡 Detective Tip: If you know what the root word means, just add "not" or "opposite" to figure out the new word!

📏 Measure 5 Things 10 min

Measurement — Inches & Centimeters

Grab a ruler 📐 and a measuring tape 🎗️ — you're going to measure 5 objects around your house!

  1. Pick 5 objects (pencil, book, shoe, spoon, toy — anything!).
  2. ESTIMATE first — guess how long you think it is.
  3. Then measure it in inches AND centimeters.
  4. Write it all down on the worksheet!
✅ Example

Object: My pencil

Estimate: 6 inches / 15 cm

Actual: 7 inches / 18 cm

Pretty close! Estimating gets easier with practice. 💪

💡 Measuring Tip: Always start at the zero mark on your ruler, not the edge! And keep the ruler flat against the object.

Shared Activity — Grammar
