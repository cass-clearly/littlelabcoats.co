☀️ Sunshine & Warmth Quiz ☀️

K-PS3 Unit 2 — Physical Science

Kindergarten · 10 Questions

📖 Ask a grown-up to read the questions aloud! 🗣️
⭐ Score: 0 / 0 Question 1 of 10
Question 1 of 10
☀️ We put a black piece of paper and a white piece of paper in the sun. Which one will feel the warmest?
Question 2 of 10
🌑 When the sun is high in the sky at noon, are the shadows SHORT or LONG?
Question 3 of 10
🌅 In the MORNING, the sun is low in the sky and shadows are very LONG. What does that tell us about how warm it is?
Question 4 of 10
🌱 We put dark soil, light sand, and water in the sun. Which warms up the FASTEST?
Question 5 of 10
🧊 We put an ice cube on a DARK plate and an ice cube on a WHITE plate — both in the sun. Which ice cube melts FIRST?
Question 6 of 10
🌳 We have two ice cubes — one in the SUN and one in the SHADE. Which one melts faster?
Question 7 of 10
🏠 You are building a shelter to keep a toy cool in the hot sun. What is the BEST color for the outside of the roof?
Question 8 of 10
💧 We put a WET cloth and a DRY cloth in the sun. The wet cloth feels cooler! Why?
Question 9 of 10
🔨 You built a shelter and tested it — but the toy inside still got warm. What should an engineer do next?
Question 10 of 10
🏡 A house has a WHITE roof and the house next door has a BLACK roof. Both are in the same sun. Which house is COOLER inside?
