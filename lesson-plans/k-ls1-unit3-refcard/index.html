🌟 All Animals Need 4 Things to Survive!
🍎 Food 💧 Water 🏠 Shelter 💨 Air
🌿 The 4 Needs — What Each One Does
🍎 FOOD Gives energy to move, grow, and stay warm Eagle hunts fish 🦅
Bear eats berries 🐻
💧 WATER Keeps bodies working, helps digest food Giraffe drinks from river 🦒
Fish breathes dissolved O₂ 🐠
🏠 SHELTER Safe from weather and predators Bird builds nest 🐦
Rabbit digs burrow 🐰
💨 AIR All animals need oxygen to breathe Dog breathes air 🐶
Fish uses gills 🐟

🌱 Living vs. Non-Living
🌱 Living Things
✅ Need food, water, air, shelter
✅ Grow and change
✅ Have babies
🌱🐱🦋🌳
🪨 Non-Living Things
❌ No needs at all
❌ Don't grow
❌ No babies
🪨🚗💺⭐
⚠️ What Happens Without Each Need?
🍎❌ No Food Animal loses energy and weakens over time
💧❌ No Water Body stops working properly
🏠❌ No Shelter Exposed to weather and predators
💨❌ No Air Most animals survive only minutes!
🐪 Camels store fat, not water — in their humps!
🐟 Fish breathe dissolved oxygen through their gills
🐧 Penguins huddle together for warmth in the cold
🐿️ Squirrels bury food to eat during winter
