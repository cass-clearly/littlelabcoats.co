🔍 Comparing Life Cycle Patterns

Grade 3 Life Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Scientists compare models to explain what stays the same and what changes across different organisms.

Key Vocabulary
  • Compare: look for similarities and differences
  • Pattern: something that repeats in a meaningful way
  • Evidence: information that supports a claim
  • Model: a representation used to explain an idea
Remember This
  • Different organisms can have different stage names.
  • The same big pattern is still birth, growth, reproduction, and death.
  • A strong comparison includes similarities, differences, and evidence.
Use It to Explain
  • Say the full pattern, not just that two organisms are “the same.”
  • Add one important difference, such as seed vs egg or gradual growth vs metamorphosis.
  • Use the comparison as evidence for your claim.
