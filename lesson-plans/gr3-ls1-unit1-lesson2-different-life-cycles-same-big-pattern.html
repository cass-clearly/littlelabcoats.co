Grade 3 LS1 Unit 1 — Lesson 2 Retired

This draft has been removed from the live lesson sequence pending a full rebuild.

Current status: the former Lesson 2 draft Different Life Cycles, Same Big Pattern is no longer part of the public Grade 3 Life Science Unit 1 sequence.

A 2026-04-14 continuity and progression review found that this page was too close to Lesson 1 and did not add enough new learning value to justify staying live.

Why it was retired

  • It repeated the same core claim, same organism set, and same compare-the-pattern task already used in Lesson 1.
  • It weakened unit progression by spending a second full lesson on the same introductory idea instead of moving into sharper evidence work.
  • It made the unit feel repetitive for families before the stronger plant, animal, and capstone lessons.

What to use instead right now

  • Keep Lesson 1 — What Is a Life Cycle? as the unit introduction.
  • Continue into Lesson 3 — Plant Life Cycles and Seeds while the replacement Lesson 2 is rebuilt.
  • The remaining live lessons in the unit are still available and remain part of the canonical package.

Reviewer note: the replacement slot should return only after it adds real novelty, clearer evidence work, and stronger progression across the unit.
