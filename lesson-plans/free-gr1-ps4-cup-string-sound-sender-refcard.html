Sound starts with vibrations, and vibrations can carry information from one place to another.

Key vocabulary
soundvibrationcommunicatemessagestringevidence
What to notice
  • Sound is made by vibrations.
  • Vibrations can move through materials.
  • Sound can communicate a message.
Materials snapshot
  • 2 paper cups
  • String or yarn
  • Paper clip or tape
  • Pencil for adult hole-starting
  • Partner
  • Worksheet
Talk-about prompts
  • What vibrated?
  • How did the message travel?
  • What changed when the string was loose?
Why it matters

A strong answer says the tight string carried vibrations better and helped the partner hear the message.
