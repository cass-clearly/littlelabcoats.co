Key vocabulary
weathersunnycloudywindyrainypattern
What to notice
  • Weather can be observed every day.
  • Symbols help young scientists record evidence.
  • Many days of evidence reveal patterns.
Materials snapshot
  • Window, doorway, or safe outdoor spot
  • Weather symbol cards or crayons
  • Thermometer optional
  • Pencil
  • Worksheet
Talk-about prompts
  • What did the sky look like?
  • What did the air feel like?
  • How might tomorrow be the same or different?
Why it matters

A strong answer uses observable evidence: sky condition, wind, and warm/cool feel. It should not rely on guesses only.

Family bridge: This card helps parents coach the evidence sentence with quick, concrete language at home.
