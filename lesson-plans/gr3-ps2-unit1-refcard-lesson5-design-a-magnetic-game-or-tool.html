🎯 Design a Magnetic Game or Tool

Grade 3 Physical Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

Engineers can use magnetic force to solve problems or make fun games.

Key Vocabulary
  • Design: make a plan for something you will build
  • Prototype: a first version
  • Criteria: what the design needs to do
Remember This
  • Start with one clear goal.
  • Build, test, and record what happened.
  • Use magnetic force on purpose, not by accident.
