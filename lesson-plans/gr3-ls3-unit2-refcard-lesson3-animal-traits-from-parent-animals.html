Animal Traits From Parent Animals Reference Card

Grade 3 · Life Science
Animal offspring often share body traits with their parents, and evidence can be collected by comparing groups.
Key vocabulary
coat patternear shapebeakcompare
What to notice
  • Repeated comparisons build stronger evidence.
  • Animal traits can be shared across a family group.
  • Scientists look for patterns, not only single examples.
Materials snapshot
  • Animal family pictures, worksheet, pencil
Talk-about prompts
  • What evidence repeated?
  • What would make your claim stronger?
  • Why compare more than one family?
Why it matters

This helps students practice data thinking inside a life-science topic they can see and discuss easily.
