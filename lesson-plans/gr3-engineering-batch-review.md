## Rachel round-1 review notes

1. **Target:** `lesson1`  
   **Observation:** The opening lesson needed a more specific challenge frame than a broad “solve a problem” introduction.  
   **Expected revision action:** Tie the unit to a concrete fragile-object protection problem and tighten the criteria language.

2. **Target:** `lesson2`  
   **Observation:** Constraints and materials needed to feel hands-on and directly connected to the unit challenge.  
   **Expected revision action:** Add more explicit material-property reasoning and challenge-specific constraint examples.

3. **Target:** `lesson3` + `lesson4`  
   **Observation:** Planning and testing needed a stronger bridge so the sketch choices clearly connect to fair-test evidence.  
   **Expected revision action:** Make the planning comparison and test-recording prompts more specific to the drop challenge.

4. **Target:** `lesson5` + `lesson6`  
   **Observation:** Redesign and defense needed stronger version-1 to version-2 reasoning, not just generic “improve” language.  
   **Expected revision action:** Emphasize failure points, targeted redesign, and evidence-based defense claims.

5. **Target:** `refcards + quiz`  
   **Observation:** Support assets were structurally aligned but still too repetitive in their explanation prompts.  
   **Expected revision action:** Make each refcard and the quiz more lesson-specific and evidence-centered.

## Margaret round-1 review notes

1. **Target:** `3-ETS1 Unit 1 lesson set`  
   **Observation:** The unit needed stronger homeschool coaching and a clearer throughline from lesson to lesson.  
   **Expected revision action:** Use a single recurring challenge and more concrete family-facing phrasing.

2. **Target:** `lesson1` + `lesson2`  
   **Observation:** Students needed simpler wording for user/need/criteria versus constraints/materials.  
   **Expected revision action:** Add concrete examples and clearer sentence frames.

3. **Target:** `lesson3` + `lesson4`  
   **Observation:** Planning and testing should give parents more support for what to watch and record.  
   **Expected revision action:** Strengthen worksheet prompts around comparison, fair testing, and observation notes.

4. **Target:** `lesson5` + `lesson6`  
   **Observation:** Final engineering explanations needed more explicit “because” and “my evidence is” language.  
   **Expected revision action:** Tighten the redesign prediction frames and final defense frames.

5. **Target:** `refcards + quiz`  
   **Observation:** The support pieces needed less repeated generic wording and more unit-specific wording.  
   **Expected revision action:** Rewrite the support copy to sound like this actual unit rather than a placeholder pattern.
