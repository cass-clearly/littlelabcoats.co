🧪 Matter Is Everywhere

Solid  ·  Liquid  ·  Gas  ·  Properties
🪨
SOLIDS
  • Keep their own shape
  • Hard, soft, rough, smooth
  • 🪨 Rocks, wood, ice
  • Can hold, stack, break
💧
LIQUIDS
  • Take container's shape
  • Flow and pour
  • 🥛 Milk, water, juice
  • Hard to hold without a container
💨
GASES
  • Fill all available space
  • Usually invisible
  • 🎈 Air in balloon
  • All around us always
🔍
PROPERTIES
  • Words to describe matter
  • Color, texture, hardness
  • Flexible or rigid?
  • Heavy or light?
⚠️
No Solids
Nothing to build with
⚠️
No Liquids
Can't drink or cook
⚠️
No Gases
No air to breathe
⚠️
No Properties
Can't describe the world
🌡️ Water can be solid, liquid AND gas on Earth
🪨 Diamonds are the hardest solid known
🫧 Soap bubbles are gas trapped in liquid film
🌊 Oceans hold 97% of Earth's water
