☀️ The Sun Gives Us Energy

Heat  ·  Light  ·  Shadows  ·  Temperature
🌡️
HEATING UP
  • Sun warms surfaces
  • Dark colors absorb more
  • 🪨 Rocks warm up fast
  • 💧 Water warms slowly
🌑
SHADOWS
  • Light can't pass through solid
  • Short at noon
  • Long at sunrise
  • Move with the sun
❄️
COOLING DOWN
  • Light colors reflect heat
  • Shade blocks sun
  • 🌳 Trees make cool spots
  • White roof = cooler house
🔧
ENGINEERING
  • Design to manage heat
  • ☂️ Umbrella = sun shelter
  • 🧢 Hat blocks UV rays
  • Test + improve!
