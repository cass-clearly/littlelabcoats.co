🏡 Designing for Local Weather

Grade 3 Earth Science — Quick Reference Card
Big Idea

A strong design solution should match the actual local weather problem it is meant to reduce.

Key Vocabulary
  • Design Solution: an idea made to solve a problem
  • Criteria: what the solution needs to do well
  • Constraint: a limit on the design
  • Local: connected to a specific place or area
Remember This
  • The best-looking design is not always the best fit for the hazard.
  • A strong solution matches the local weather conditions and the real problem.
  • Comparing more than one solution helps you decide which has more merit.
