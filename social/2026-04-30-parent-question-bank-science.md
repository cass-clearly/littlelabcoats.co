## Notes on tone
- Parents are often asking for science that is fun, doable, low-prep, and not intimidating.
- They want real learning, but they are also tired.
- Posts that say "you do not need to be a science expert" should perform well.
- Book lists and low-prep experiment lists are likely strong save-content.
- The strongest conversion bridge is: common parent pain point -> simple framework -> printable/lesson inside LLC.
