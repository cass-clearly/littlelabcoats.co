Keep These Science Supplies on Hand

Not because you need a giant science closet — just because these few things make it ridiculously easier to say “yes” to a fun experiment.

This is the real starter kit.

If you have these around the house, you can pull off a surprising amount of science without panic-buying weird supplies at 9pm.
