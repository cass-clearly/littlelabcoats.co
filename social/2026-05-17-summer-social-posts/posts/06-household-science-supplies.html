Useful Items to Keep Around the House for Science

You do not need fancy supplies. A small stash of everyday materials can carry weeks of meaningful science play.

Keep these handy

  • tape
  • toilet roll tubes
  • baking soda + vinegar
  • food coloring
  • paper plates

Also useful

  • string
  • craft sticks
  • droppers
  • magnifying glass
  • measuring cups
