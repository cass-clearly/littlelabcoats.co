Top 10 Tips for Homeschool Science

The goal is not a perfect lab. It is curious kids, a simple plan, and space to notice what happens.

Remember this

  • keep it short
  • use what you have
  • let kids predict
  • repeat favorite experiments
  • go outside often

And this

  • ask better questions
  • embrace a little mess
  • write down observations
  • link science to real life
  • stop before burnout
