5 STEM Activities for Kindergarten

Low-prep, hands-on science ideas with actual directions a parent can use right away.

Grab-and-go format.

Each activity tells you what to grab, what to do, and what your child gets to notice or test.

1

Ramp Roll Rescue

books + cardboard + toy car compare speed
Do this

Build a low ramp and a steep ramp. Roll the same car down both and compare what happens.

Ask
  • Which ramp is faster?
  • Which one goes farther?
2

Sink or Float Bowl

bowl of water + 6 objects predict and test
Do this

Gather small objects, predict first, then test each one in water.

Ask
  • What surprised you?
  • Did every heavy item sink?
3

Magnet Hunt

magnet + household items sort evidence
Do this

Test items around the house and sort them into sticks and does not stick.

Ask
  • What do the sticking items have in common?
4

Leaf Detective Walk

3 leaves + outdoor space observe and compare
Do this

Collect three leaves and compare size, color, shape, and texture.

Ask
  • Which is biggest?
  • Which feels smoothest?
5

Paper Bridge Rescue

paper + 2 cups + pennies/books build and improve
Do this

Test a flat sheet between two cups, then fold or roll it and test again. Which design holds more weight?

Ask
  • What changed?
  • How did the new shape help?

Parent tip

You do not need all five. Pick one, keep it short, and let the conversation be the win.
