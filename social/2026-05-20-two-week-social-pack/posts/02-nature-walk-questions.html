10 Questions to Ask on a Nature Walk

A good science lesson can start with one walk, one good question, and a child who notices something weird, beautiful, or surprising.

Questions to ask

  • What do you notice first?
  • What looks different here?
  • What might live under that?
  • Why do you think that plant grows there?
  • What changed since last time?

Keep going with…

  • What is making that sound?
  • Which surface feels warmest?
  • What patterns do you see?
  • What would you sketch to remember this?
  • What do you want to find out later?
Nature walks get better when you bring better questions.
