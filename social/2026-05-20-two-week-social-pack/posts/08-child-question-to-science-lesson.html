7 Ways to Turn a Child’s Question into a Science Lesson

The best science lessons often start with a child asking something slightly inconvenient at exactly the right time.

When they ask “why…”

  • slow down
  • repeat the question
  • ask what they think first
  • test one small part

Then try this

  • observe together
  • compare two examples
  • write down what changed
A child’s question is often the lesson plan trying to introduce itself.
