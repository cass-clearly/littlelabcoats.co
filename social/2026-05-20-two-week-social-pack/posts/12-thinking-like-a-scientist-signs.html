5 Signs Your Child Is Already Thinking Like a Scientist

It often shows up long before a polished notebook page does — in questions, comparisons, patterns, and stubborn little experiments of their own.

You might notice…

  • they ask follow-up questions
  • they compare what changed
  • they repeat things on purpose

And also…

  • they look for patterns
  • they test their own ideas
  • they want to show you evidence
Science thinking often looks wonderfully ordinary before it looks academic.
