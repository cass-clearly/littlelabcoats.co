About the founder

Hi, I'm Serena. 👋

I'm a homeschool mom of three — and I love science! I look forward to teaching it. But it takes me so much time.

For every topic I teach, I piece together materials from multiple sources: YouTube, Generation Genius, Teachers Pay Teachers, books from Amazon, random websites. It. Takes. Time.

And at the end of the lesson, my kids understand the concepts — but we have nothing to show for it. No paper trail, nothing for audits or assessments, and we did nothing hands-on. That made me sad.

Wasn't the point of science to get your hands dirty? To feel it? To live it? To experience it? Because that's how kids learn. They don't learn from long paragraphs of theory, and a video will only take them so far.

And so Little Lab Coats was born.

I happen to have a First Class Degree in Zoology, a Master's from Oxford in Integrative Bioscience, and a PhD in Microbiology. I've spent my career doing real science — asking questions, running experiments, testing ideas. I brought all of that into building curriculum that actually works for families like mine.

Science changed how I see the world. I want to give that to your kids too.

🏡 Homeschool Mom of Three 🎓 First Class Degree in Zoology 🏛️ MSc Oxford, Integrative Bioscience 🔬 PhD Microbiology
