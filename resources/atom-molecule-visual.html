Atom & molecule visual

A clear one-page visual showing what atoms are, how molecules form, and why H₂O means two hydrogen atoms bonded to one oxygen atom.

  • Atom parts are shown as a model, not to scale.
  • Uses real SVG labels for crisp printing.
  • Includes water, oxygen, and carbon dioxide examples.

Printing tip: Atoms are far too small to see with eyes or classroom microscopes. Use this poster as a model for talking about parts and patterns, not as an exact picture of size or distance.
