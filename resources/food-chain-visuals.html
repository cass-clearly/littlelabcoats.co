Food chain visuals

Two simple food chain examples with producer, consumer, predator, and decomposer language, plus a clear note that arrows show energy movement.

  • Arrows show energy moving from food to eater.
  • Includes land and ocean examples.
  • Adds decomposers without making the page chaotic.

Printing tip: A food chain is a simplified model. Real ecosystems are food webs, with many overlapping feeding relationships.
