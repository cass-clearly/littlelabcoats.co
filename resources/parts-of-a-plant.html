Parts of a plant

A labeled flowering plant diagram with the job of each major part: roots, stem, leaves, flower, fruit, and seeds.

  • Labels plant parts and their jobs.
  • Simple enough for young learners but accurate for reference.
  • Pairs well with seed sprouting and garden walks.

Printing tip: Not every plant has showy flowers or fruit you can easily see at the same time. Use this as a basic flowering plant model, then compare real plants nearby.
